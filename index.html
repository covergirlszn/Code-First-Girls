Hello, We Are The Care Project

We aim to promote self care in a society where everyone is always on the go. It is important to take time out of your day to recentre and relax. This could be through breathing exercises, yoga or simply just taking a nap. Self care is a multifarious concept, and we are here to break it down so that you can reflect and recalibate. This is your quiet corner on the internet. Everyone is invited! Enjoy your stay.
